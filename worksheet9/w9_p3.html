1. Compare projection shadows to shadow mapping by listing advantages and disadvantages of the two techniques.

    2. The pros of projection shadows are that it has a easy implementation and it is inexpensive to compute. However, it has the following cons: it works better on flat surfaces and it is difficult to use it in non-flat surfaces, and controlling the colour and transparency of the shadow is also hard because it is a compressed version of the object and not a real shadow.

    Meanwhile, the shadow mapping pro is that each object that can be rendered can cast shadows and does not depend on finding the silhouette for the object. But the con is that it is more difficult to implement and it requires more computational power as it needs two different renders.
